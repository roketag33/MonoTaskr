Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

TL;DR: MonoTaskr stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information or usage data. Your privacy is our top priority.

1. Introduction

MonoTaskr ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how MonoTaskr handles your information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

2. Data Collection

MonoTaskr does NOT collect any personal data. The extension operates entirely on your local device using Chrome's local storage API. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Track your browsing history
  • Monitor which websites you visit
  • Record your session data beyond local storage
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Transmit any data to external servers

3. Data Storage

MonoTaskr stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Timer State: Current session status, duration, and remaining time
  • Session History: Your last 50 completed focus sessions (timestamp and duration)
  • Settings: Your customized list of blocked websites
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you've completed the initial setup

This data is stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API and remains on your device. It is never sent to us or any third parties.

4. Permissions

MonoTaskr requests the following Chrome permissions to function:

  • storage: To save your settings and session history locally on your device
  • alarms: To countdown the focus timer and trigger notifications
  • notifications: To notify you when a focus session completes
  • activeTab: To display the blocking overlay on tabs with blocked websites

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and are not used for tracking or data collection.

5. Third-Party Services

MonoTaskr does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. The extension operates completely offline after installation.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage APIs, your data is protected by your device's security measures and Chrome's built-in protections.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all data stored by MonoTaskr at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension from Chrome, or
  • Clearing your browser's extension data via Chrome Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Hosted app data

8. Children's Privacy

MonoTaskr does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension can be used by anyone without providing any personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of MonoTaskr after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Open Source

MonoTaskr is open source software. You can review the source code to verify our privacy practices at: GitHub Repository

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or MonoTaskr's privacy practices, please contact us at: privacy@monotaskr.com
